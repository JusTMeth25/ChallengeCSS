Yosemite National Park

Yosemite Falls, located in California’s Sierra Nevada mountains, is one of the United States' most iconic natural preserves and a UNESCO World Heritage site. Spanning nearly 1,200 square miles, it is world-renowned for its dramatic granite cliffs, massive waterfalls, and ancient giant sequoias.

Yosemite National Park is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, offering a wide range of activities such as hiking, rock climbing, camping, and wildlife viewing. The park's diverse ecosystems support a rich variety of flora and fauna, making it a paradise for nature lovers and photographers alike.
