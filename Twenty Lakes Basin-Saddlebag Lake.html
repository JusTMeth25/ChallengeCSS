Twenty Lakes Basin-Saddlebag Lake

Saddlebag Lake and the Twenty Lakes Basin are premier alpine destinations in the Eastern Sierra, located just outside Yosemite’s Tioga Pass entrance. At an elevation of over 10,000 feet, the area offers a dramatic landscape of granite peaks, glacial lakes, and wildflower meadows.

The region offers excellent opportunities for hiking, fishing, and wildlife observation. The lakes are surrounded by dense forests and offer stunning views of the surrounding peaks.
