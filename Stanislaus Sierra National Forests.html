Stanislaus & Sierra National Forests

Stanislaus and Sierra National Forests are vast wilderness areas in California’s Sierra Nevada mountains, known for their pristine lakes, dense forests, and diverse wildlife. These forests offer endless opportunities for outdoor recreation, including hiking, camping, fishing, and wildlife observation.

The forests are home to a variety of plant and animal species, many of which are unique to the region. Visitors can explore scenic trails, discover hidden waterfalls, and experience the tranquility of untouched natural landscapes.
