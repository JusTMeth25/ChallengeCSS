Virginia Lakes-Summit Lake

Virginia Lakes and Summit Lake are nestled in the scenic Sierra Nevada mountains of California. These pristine alpine lakes offer breathtaking views and are surrounded by lush forests and rugged terrain.

The hike from Virginia Lakes to Summit Lake is a scenic 10–12 mile round-trip trek in the Hoover Wilderness (near Lee Vining, CA), featuring alpine scenery, multiple lakes, and high-elevation views. The trail rises from around 9,000 to over 10,000 feet, passing Frog Lakes before reaching Summit Lake, which sits near the Yosemite National Park border.
